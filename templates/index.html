Our Mission

In the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic, most colleges and universities have decided to go remote. Even in the schools that haven't, some students have decided to go remote in fear of contracting and spreading the virus. One of the key benefits of going to college is the ability to meet others and network. With the move from in-person education to remote learning, it has become significantly harder for students to meet new friends. Our goal was to create an environment where people can meet others from their school and classes and potentially create lifelong friendships.
